I'm an Assistant Professor in the Dept. of Economics at CUNEF Universidad, in Madrid since September 2020.

I received my Ph.D. in Economics from CEMFI in July 2019, under the supervision of Profs. Nezih Guner and Josep Pijoan-Mas.

My research lies within the area of quantitative macroeconomics. I'm interested in issues related to family economics and fiscal policy. I'm also interested in the macroeconomic implications of frictions such as firing costs and price/wage stickiness.
